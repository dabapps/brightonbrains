About

Brighton is full of creative and technical people, who organise free groups and meetings to share knowledge and collaborate.

This website helps publicise the creative, digital and STEM communities in Brighton and the surrounding areas.

Get involved

If you know of any other groups that should be on this page, or have any suggestions for any other improvements to this site, please let us know by emailing us.

Alternatively, you can create an issue (or, even better, fork the project and create a pull request) on GitHub.
